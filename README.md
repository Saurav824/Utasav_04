# 🌏 UTSAV – AI-Driven Cultural Heritage & Artisan Empowerment Platform

## 📌 Overview
**UTSAV** is an AI-powered digital platform designed to bridge the gap between traditional cultural heritage and modern technology. The platform focuses on empowering artisans, fostering student innovation, and promoting sustainable economic growth while preserving India's rich cultural identity.

It provides a unified ecosystem integrating:
- Artisans
- Students
- Consumers
- AI-driven analytics

---

## 🎯 Problem Statement
Traditional crafts and heritage industries face multiple challenges:
- Limited digital presence of artisans
- Lack of market access and insights
- Fragmented cultural data
- Minimal collaboration between students and artisans
- Absence of AI-driven decision-making tools

---

## 💡 Solution
UTSAV addresses these issues by offering:
- A centralized digital platform
- AI-based recommendation system
- Cultural heritage mapping
- Innovation collaboration between students and artisans
- Data-driven insights for market trends and sustainability

---

## 🧠 Key Features

### 🗺️ Heritage Mapping Module
- Explore cultural assets region-wise
- Structured data on crafts, traditions, and materials

### 👨‍🎨 Artisan Dashboard
- Product upload & management
- Performance tracking
- Market analytics

### 🎓 Student Innovation Portal
- Submit ideas for modernizing traditional crafts
- Collaboration with artisans

### 🤖 AI Innovation Engine
- Product improvement suggestions
- Demand forecasting
- Sustainability scoring
- Market trend analysis

### 🛒 Marketplace Integration
- Direct artisan-to-consumer interaction
- Reduced dependency on intermediaries

## 🏗️ System Architecture

The platform follows a layered architecture:
- **Frontend (UI/UX)** – Interactive dashboards
- **Backend** – API & business logic
- **Database** – Cloud-based storage
- **AI Module** – Analytics & predictions

Key Technologies:
- React.js (Frontend)
- Node.js / Express.js (Backend)
- MongoDB / Firebase / Supabase (Database)
- TensorFlow / ML Models (AI Engine)
